## 1. The "Success Gap" Analysis

Here is what is currently missing from your plan to ensure a dominant market position:

### The "Aha!" Moment (Product-Market Fit)

In 2026, users suffer from "Data Fatigue."

- **What's Missing:** A "Scenario Simulator."
- **The Feature:** Instead of just showing current equity, allow users to toggle variables: _"What happens to my cash flow if interest rates rise by 0.5%?"_ or _"If I renovate the kitchen for $20k, what is the projected Rent-on-Equity (ROE)?"_
- **Validation:** You have PMF when users use the app _before_ they make a financial decision, not just after.

### Hyper-Local & ESG Transparency

The market has shifted toward "Ethical Investing" and "Climate Resilience."

- **Expansion Point:** Integrate APIs that provide **Flood/Fire Risk Scores** and **Energy Efficiency (EPC) ratings**.
- **Value:** Investors are now terrified of uninsurable assets. Being the app that flags "High Risk" or "Low Efficiency" creates immediate trust that giants like Zillow often gloss over.
